Housing Costs

[House keys image]

Keep housing under 30% of income. Consider roommates, downsizing, or moving to cheaper areas.

Food Shopping

[Shopping cart image]

Plan meals, buy generic brands, and shop seasonal produce. Can save $100+ monthly.

Reduce Bills

[Light bulb image]

Switch to LED bulbs, unplug electronics, adjust thermostat by 2 degrees. Save $30-50 monthly on utilities.
